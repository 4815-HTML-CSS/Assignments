Welcome to My Food Website!

Here's an overview of what kinds of foods you can find in Colombia:

  • Caribbean area
    • Arroz con coco (coconut rice)
    • Cazuela de marisccos (seafood casserole)
    • Mote de queso (cheese soup)
  • Amazon area
    • Pirarucu (big fish from the amazon river up to 4 meters)
    • Mojojoy (worm)
    • Ceviche de pintadillo (fish from the amazon)
  • Coffee-Growing area
    • Bandeja paisa (dish with beans rice egg and many other things)
    • Mondongo (tripe soup)
    • Trucha (fish from very clean and very cold water)
